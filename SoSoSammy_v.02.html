My Baking and Cooking

I enjoy baking and cooking in my free time. I love creating delicious masterpieces and decorating and plating them beautifully. I prefer to bake, although I do cook. Some things that I enjoy baking are cookies, pies, bread, and other sweet treats. Here are some of the recipes I made:

Recipe I Made How it Went Picture
Wheat Bread I baked this whole-wheat bread using a family recipe. I halved it since the last time I made it, it made three loaves of bread. I made one loaf of bread this time. I proofed my dough in the oven with a pan of boiling water, which helped it to rise nicely. Then, in the oven, it rose even more. When I took my bread out of the oven, it was super huge. I took it out of the pan and let it cool. Sadly, it collapsed in on itself because it was too big. The middle of the bread was doughy; it didn't get cooked evenly. The top was nice and crispy though. If I were to make this recipe again, I would half the recipe, and put the dough into two loaf pans.
Apple Pie I made this apple pie using a grain-free all-purpose flour blend. I used monkfruit as the sweetener. The crust for the pie was crumbly; it was hard to roll it out and transfer it to the pie tin. I suspect this was because of the flour I used. I tried my best, and performed intense pie surgery when needed. While the pie was baking, the house filled with such a delicious smell. My family loved the pie, and said it tasted amazing!
Strawberry Cupcakes I made these delicious keto strawberry cupcakes a while back. I worked super hard on them, and I took my time to make it perfect. The cupcakes turned out amazing, and the frosting was absolutely to die for! (Figuratively, hehehe) My family loved these cupcakes.
