My best cooking and baking
Recipe I made How it went Picture
Apple pie I made this for my Food and Nutrition 2 class. I used a grain-free all-purpose flour blend, so the crust was crumbly and didn't stick together. I tried my best, and ended up doing intense pie surgery to make sure the crust stayed together. Luckily, the taste and smell wasn't affected! My family loved this pie.
Whole wheat bread I also made this whole wheat bread for my Food and Nutrition 2 class. I used a special technique for proofing my bread, which made it rise nice and big. Sadly, it caved in on itself after cooling on the counter after it finished baking. It was still delicious though!
Mini waffles I made these mini waffles for my sister. I used Kodiak Cakes mix, and I took the time to plate them all nice and beautiful. They turned out amazing and delicious!
